My story

During the Corona pandemic in 2020, I got my first smartphone for online learning. I was curious about how Android apps work. I did study and came to know that they can be made using Java. So I started learning Java. Using my phone, I practiced coding on Java for a few months and then moved on to learn about Android App Development. Making apps on a phone was tricky, but my curiosity kept me going. I also learned about HTML, CSS, and JS at that time. In December 2021, I got a laptop. Starting in 2022, I dived deeper into the world of programming, polishing my Android skills and learning more technologies.
